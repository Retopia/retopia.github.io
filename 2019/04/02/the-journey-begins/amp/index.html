Wow can you believe it? It’s April already, time really passes so fast!

Hello everyone, welcome to my blog I created out of boredom. I doubt I’ll share this blog with anyone else, but I’ll be writing here with the formality and privacy that would be expected if I were. I realize that I get bored a lot on breaks and weekends, so why not use that time to practice my writing! On this blog, there will be rants, opinions, and maybe short story. Hopefully as time goes on, the improvement in my writing will be evident here!

– PT
